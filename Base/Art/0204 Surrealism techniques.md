# Surrealist techniques in Arts
Суть сурреализма - в поиске подсознательного. 

*Automatism*
Automatic drawing, for instance, involved allowing the pencil to move freely across the paper without conscious control, revealing hidden emotions and ideas.

*Juxtoposition* 
Совмещение несовместимого. Совмещая несовместимое артисты провоцировали эмоциональный и интеллектуальный реакции, дизориентацию.

*Transformation*
Трансформация знакомых в вещей в нечто другое. Тем самым артисты затуманив границу между реальностью и подсознательным. Артисты хотели показать скрытые желания, сны и призывая зрителей приходить со своими интепритациями. 

*Collage*
Collage, a technique widely used by surrealist artists, involved combining various materials and images to create new compositions. By incorporating elements from disparate sources, artists aimed to disrupt conventional narratives and create unexpected associations. Collage allowed them to break free from the constraints of reality, forging unique visual narratives that explored the depths of the human psyche.

*Decalcomania*
Decalcomania, a technique developed by surrealist artists, involved pressing wet paint between two surfaces and then separating them to create unpredictable textures and patterns. This spontaneous and unpredictable process resulted in abstract formations that suggested multiple interpretations. The use of decalcomania further emphasized the surrealists’ interest in exploring the subconscious and the unpredictable nature of the creative process.
